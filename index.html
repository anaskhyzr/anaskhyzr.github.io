About

I operate at the intersection of two things most people treat as opposites — software engineering and cinematic storytelling.

On the technical side: cross-platform apps, full-stack systems, clean backend logic. On the creative side: silent films, vertical series, and visual content where composition does what dialogue cannot.

My filmmaking brand is VISIARCH — Vision as Architecture. Silence as Language. Every frame is designed to carry meaning without narration.
